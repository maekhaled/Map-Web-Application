Welcome to the Mapping Travel App!

Double click anywhere on the map to add a marker.

To change the location of a marker, you can simply drag and drop it in the desired location.

To update properties of a marker, click on the marker, select "Edit Marker", make the changes to the desired fields, and then submit your changes by clicking "Update Marker".

To delete a marker, simply click on the marker and click "Delete Marker".
